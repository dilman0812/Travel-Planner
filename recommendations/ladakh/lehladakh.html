LADAKH                

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Leh

  • Arrive at Leh airport.
  • Meet with the representative and transfer to your reserved accommodation.
  • Check in to the hotel and get proper rest to acclimatize.
  • Overnight stay and dinner at Leh.

Day 2: Exploring Leh

  • After breakfast, proceed for an excursion to Sangam - the confluence of Zanskar & Indus Rivers.
  • Visit the Magnetic hill and Gurudwara Pather Sahib.
  • Later in the evening visit Leh Market, Leh Palace, and Shanti Stupa.
  • Overnight stay and dinner at Leh.

Day 3: Transfer to Nubra Valley

  • After breakfast, drive to Nubra Valley via Khardungla (the highest motorable road in the world at 18,380 ft).
  • You can take short breaks at roadside cafes and scenic points for photography.
  • On arrival, check in to the hotel/camp.
  • Post lunch, enjoy a camel safari (double-humped camels, which are found only in Ladakh & Mongolia) at the Sand Dunes between Deskit and Hundur Village.
  • Overnight stay and dinner at the camp.

Day 4: Transfer to Pangong Lake

  • After breakfast, leave for Pangong Lake.
  • Check in to the camp post-arrival.
  • Day at leisure to enjoy the scenic beauty of the landscape.
  • Overnight stay and dinner at the camp.

Day 5: Transfer to Leh

  • Early morning, enjoy the picturesque view of the sunrise on the lake.
  • After breakfast, drive back to Leh.
  • Visit the Thiksey Monastery and Shey Palace which lies en route.
  • Overnight stay and dinner in Leh.

Day 6: Departure

  • Drop off at Leh airport for your onward journey.
