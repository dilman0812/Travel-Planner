JAMMU & KASHMIR

Jammu & Kashmir, often referred to as "J&K," is a northern region in India known for its diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage. It includes the Kashmir Valley, Jammu, and Ladakh. J&K is famous for its stunning natural beauty, including the Himalayan mountains, serene lakes, and lush valleys. It's also renowned for its historic temples, such as Vaishno Devi, and its traditional handicrafts like Pashmina shawls.
